About Us

Your Home Away
From Home

JSR Residency is a premium guest house nestled in the heart of Rohini, New Delhi — offering a refined and peaceful stay experience for business travelers, families, and long-term guests.

Conveniently located near Rajiv Gandhi Cancer Hospital in Sector 5, Rohini, we provide clean, comfortable, and affordable accommodations with personalised service.

Whether you are here for a short visit or an extended stay, our dedicated team ensures every moment is comfortable and memorable.
